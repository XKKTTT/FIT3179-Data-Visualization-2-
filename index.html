Global Travels into Malaysia

With rich traditions, diverse landscapes, and thriving urban centers, Malaysia has long been a cultural melting pot and a top destination for global travelers. This visualisation explores how international hotel guests have shaped its tourism over the years. Explore the data and discover insights into the growing influx of visitors to various states in Malaysia.

States by Arrivals per Kilometer Squared

Except during Covid in 2021, Penang has the highest arrivals per kilometer squared, with an average of 3676.4 across all 4 years. Pahang is the state with the lowest arrivals per km squared at an average of 0.1225 across all 4 years

States with the Highest Number of Hotel Guests

Kuala Lumpur is the region with the highest number of hotel guests. The peak was 2019 with over 10 million recorded guests.
